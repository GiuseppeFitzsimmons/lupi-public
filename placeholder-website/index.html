Lupi is an ERP1 SaaS2 for the fashion industry.

Its simple but vital role is digital representation and management of all the components of the production life-cycle of a collection...

...from prototyping to production, including fabric inventory, order management, and timelines.

Lupi features an economical, renewable subscription model, forecasting, simulations, and user management.

Beta subscriptions are available now and Lupi will be managing its first commercial collections in winter of 2022.
